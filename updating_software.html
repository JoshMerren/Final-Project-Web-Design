Keeping Your Software Up To Date

Are you Staying Updated?

Software updates are important and necessary to protect yourself from cybercrimes. Software updates can fix security vulnerabilities and minimize risks. They also enhance the way the software runs. Updates also replace outdated technology and add new features. Remember, cybercriminals are looking for easy targets. By updating your devices you are safeguarding your data and protecting yourself from cybercrime and hackers that are targeting you. Most manufacturers patch their software often. It is important to have your devices set up to receive these updates automatically. This protects you from cybercrime by patching the holes that cybercriminals use to infect your device and install malicious hardware.
